Programming Style: Miscellaneous

  • Spaces around operators, after keywords, but not after method names
    Good: if (x > Math.sqrt(y))
    Bad: if(x>Math.sqrt (y))
  • Don't use C-style arrays:
    Good: int[] numbers
    Bad: int numbers[]
  • No magic numbers
    Good: h = HASH_MULTIPLIER * h + val[off];
    Bad: h = 31 * h + val[off];
